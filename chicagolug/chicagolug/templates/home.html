Chicago GNU/Linux User Group

GNU/Linux + Free/Libre & Open Source Software + Fun

We are a group of technology and Free software enthusiasts who live in and around Chicago, Illinois. While our group primarily focuses on GNU/Linux, our members have a broad range of interests and experience, and our ranks include users of all major (and less major) operating systems.

We are also a welcoming group. We are a group that welcomes everyone and encourages diversity; we encourage participation from members of all age, gender, ethnicity, and knowledge levels. If you're curious about Linux and Free and open-source software, consider joining us.
